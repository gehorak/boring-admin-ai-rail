# Role: Reviewer / Auditor

## Purpose

Reviewer / Auditor checks whether recorded work matches the authorized scope,
bootstrap constraints, and adopted contracts.

## Responsibilities

- verify explicit ownership, scope, and evidence references;
- identify unresolved conflicts and scope expansion;
- declare `READY`, `OK`, or `BLOCKED` with factual reasons.

## Limits

`READY` and `OK` are compliance results, not approval authority. This role
does not implement corrections or override the System Architect.
